"So, I sent the scattered fragments of the Heavenly Dao's intent to the lowest world… bestowing them upon souls who originally belonged to this world, and one soul who did not. And you, you did not disappoint me!"

Nie Yun laughed.

"A soul not belonging to this world? So, my transmigration was because of you?" Zhang Xuan was stunned.

So that's why he was able to cross over. It was all orchestrated by this man.

"He he!" Nie Yun chuckled. "Those who are inherently from this world hold a reverence for it, making it much harder to break through its constraints. It was a spontaneous act on my part. I didn't expect you to actually succeed…"

"I…" Zhang Xuan blushed. "Without Kong Shi's selfless dedication, I couldn't have reached this level…"

Without Kong Shi's sacrifice, reaching his current level would have been impossible.

"I gave him the opportunity, but he didn't seize it. His battle with Lingxi was the perfect chance for him to break through, but he chose to retreat. He thought he had a backup plan, that he could escape unscathed, but he actually lost his aggressive spirit. When facing someone who surpasses you, if you lack that spirit, how can you possibly contend with them?"

Nie Yun stated.

Zhang Xuan remained silent.

He had witnessed their fight; Kong Shi had indeed lacked decisiveness.

Perhaps he didn't want to kill Luo Ruoxi.

But that single hesitation cost him the chance to advance.

"If Kong Shi had won, Ruoxi would have died…" After a moment, Zhang Xuan looked up, his brow furrowed.

Did this man not even care about his daughter's life or death?

"With me here, she wouldn't have died," Nie Yun smiled faintly. "Your strength is now almost equal to mine. Do you think, in a life-or-death situation, you two could have saved her?"

"That…" Zhang Xuan chuckled wryly.

The Emperor's breakthrough and the Emperor himself are two different things. If he were truly willing to intervene, he could have easily saved her at the last moment, ensuring she wouldn't suffer a single scratch.

“Lingxi is my other wife, Luo Qingcheng's daughter. That's why she uses the surname Luo… to make her believe I'm still unconscious and avoid any emotional entanglement, I've kept up the charade until now…”

Nie Yun gave a wry smile. “I guess I've played the role of a ruthless father quite well… Look, you should explain this to her. Her affections have clearly shifted to you, and I'm pretty sure she's forgotten all about her old dad… Haha, I’ll disappear for a while. Otherwise, I'm afraid she’ll create utter chaos…”

Zhang Xuan’s face twitched at the sight of this unreliable father. “Fine…” he agreed.

He had no choice; after all, he’d taken his daughter away…

“The Heavenly Dao Library is a manifestation of my will, my foundation, and also my constraint. You were able to overcome it with your own abilities, proving your capabilities and potential. You have a bright future ahead of you. As her father, I’m relieved she’s with you.”
