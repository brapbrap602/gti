  • With safety assured, Li Fan did his best to maintain the Innate Grandmaster Technique, simultaneously focusing his attention on the True Immortal blood that resembled a fiery wall. Under the impact of countless divine senses, its defenses seemed to show faint cracks. The stalemate continued for an unknown duration. Finally, Li Fan broke through the defenses of the True Immortal blood. The backlash vanished out of thin air, and his soul instantly felt clear and cool. A beam of light emerged from the True Immortal blood, enveloping Li Fan's divine sense. Afterward, a segment of light and shadow appeared before him. It was as if it were covered in mist, and all surrounding scenes were indistinct. An enraged voice was heard from a distant, towering figure. It was sporadic, and only the general meaning could be understood: "...Qionglang Star Domain... Yan... guarding..." "...Target found..." "Your subject obeys the order." A low voice emanated from his own body. The light and shadow shifted again. In the next moment, he was already within the Xuanhuang Realm. Standing high above, overlooking the myriad sentient beings within the realm. However, his body's attention seemed not to be on the Xuanhuang Realm below, but rather on the outside of the starry sky. He appeared to be waiting for something. After an unknown amount of time, a beam of light suddenly arrived from beyond the heavens. His gaze immediately locked onto it. But he did not interfere, allowing the beam of light to fall into the Xuanhuang Realm. Only when the light completely dimmed and settled did his figure descend to the mortal world to investigate specifically. "Not yet." "But it's not bad. If it were years ago, ascension would have been possible. What a pity..." The figure seemed accustomed to this outcome, shaking his head with neither joy nor sorrow, and then returned to the Xuanhuang Realm's celestial dome. Silently watching, waiting for the arrival of the next target... The scene abruptly froze, then rapidly faded and dissipated like a vintage photograph. Li Fan jolted back to reality, returning to the present. Although the memories experienced within the True Immortal blood were fleeting, Li Fan felt as if he had lived through dozens of lifetimes. This longevity was also reflected in his physical body. His long hair, which had been flowing freely, had now grown to his waist, interspersed with white strands. His face, palms, and even the skin all over his body had become somewhat wrinkled. Li Fan looked at his age on the Return to Reality panel. His mental age had not changed, but his physiological lifespan limit had been directly reduced to 50 years. He was still thirty years old in spirit, yet his life was nearing its end. "Is this the price of prying into True Immortals?" After attempting to use the Xuanhuang Realm's celestial treasures that increased lifespan and confirming they could not increase this reduced lifespan limit, Li Fan's heart filled with dread. "If it were an ordinary cultivator, they would surely be driven to a desperate situation by this." "Fortunately, I have [Return to Reality]. A 50-year lifespan limit makes little difference to me." "There are still 20 years left in this life, which is enough to complete my plans." Li Fan's mood calmed down, and he couldn't help but recall the scenes he had seen within the True Immortal blood earlier. "The True Immortal who owned this blood should have been ordered to come to Xuanhuang to find a certain target..." "The word 'subject' is quite interesting." "Moreover, that beam of light from beyond the heavens..." Li Fan narrowed his eyes. Combining the information from the ancient immortal formations in the Xuanhuang Realm and the clues obtained from outside the high wall, a guess emerged in Li Fan's mind: "The ancient Xuanhuang Realm was also 'capturing' things." "Unlike the later capture of other cultivation worlds, the target at that time was... 'Humans'?" Li Fan's eyes flickered. "But it's not bad. If it were years ago, ascension would have been possible..." Li Fan repeated the True Immortal's words several times. "The 'light from beyond the heavens' that had the 'potential for ascension' was not the target that True Immortal was waiting for. What exactly is it..." Suddenly, Li Fan, for some unknown reason, began to associate himself with it. Although the True Immortal's memories were vague and from countless years ago, Li Fan inexplicably felt a sense of having narrowly escaped a catastrophe. When he came to his senses, his back was already drenched in cold sweat. This was not due to Li Fan's timidity, but rather a primal fear and relief after realizing the situation in hindsight. "This..." His rapidly beating heart slowly calmed down. Li Fan temporarily left the underground space of the Dao Discussion Platform and flickered back to the sky above the Great Profound Minor World. Boundless clouds obscured the sight of the living beings on the ground. Li Fan savored the premonition he had just experienced. "Although it's hard to believe..." "But a premonition from the depths of my being tells me that years ago, the True Immortal's target in Xuanhuang might actually have been me?!" "Or rather, not me, but [Return to Reality]!" Li Fan closed his eyes, preventing his inner turmoil and shock from being exposed. "[Return to Reality], [Return to Reality]..." As he spoke, the surrounding scenery became illusory and ethereal again. Being isolated from the real world gave Li Fan a slight sense of peace. "[Return to Reality] involves the Great Dao of [True and False] and [Illusion and Reality]." "Wondrous artifacts of heaven and earth, True Immortal script characters, and even the mysterious symbol that created the Xuanhuang Great Heavenly Venerate's bloodline, are all partial projections of the Great Dao." "But only [Return to Reality] stands above all else." "It can be said to be almost the embodiment of the Great Dao of [True and False] and [Illusion and Reality] itself..." Li Fan's thoughts churned as he calmly contemplated. "The fact that the True Immortal called himself 'subject' suggests that the towering figure in the memory must have an extraordinary status." "Qionglang Star Domain... Yan..." Li Fan attempted to deduce the possible truth of the matter from the fragmented information. "It appears the Immortal Realm was already facing a crisis at that time." "Yan..." "With the power of a True Immortal, perhaps they could deduce a solution to the Immortal Realm's crisis, which was [Return to Reality]." "That's why they were 'lying in wait' in the Xuanhuang Realm?" "However, for some reason, I arrived here tens of thousands of years too late." ... After breaking free from the True Immortal's memories for a long time, the lingering sense of narrowly escaping death in Li Fan's heart eventually dissipated. With his emotions calmed, Li Fan re-examined his earlier speculation and found it almost absurdly laughable. "What is the truth?" Li Fan, his mood having calmed, flickered from the sky back to the underground. He looked at the remaining True Immortal blood in the golden net, pondering for a long time. "I cannot pry into it further in this life." "I will see in the next life if I can find more clues." "Even if my guess is confirmed, it doesn't matter. After all, the Immortal Realm faced a great calamity, and those True Immortals who wanted to 'hunt' me have already disappeared first!" A glint flashed in Li Fan's eyes, and his state of mind became steady once more. Although he could not further pry into the memories from the True Immortal blood in this life, it did not prevent Li Fan from continuing his research on the True Immortal flesh. "Next, let me see how the True Immortal flesh resists the power of True Immortal script characters." With his Origin Force Essence surgical knife, he cut off a small piece of flesh.
