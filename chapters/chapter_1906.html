The Scripture Treasure refers to the true scriptures of the Daoist Canon and all Daoist arts. The Master Treasure refers to the past sages of the Daoist tradition and all those who have attained the Dao. One of the most important precepts of the Daoist tradition, the 'Three Refuges' within the Three Refuges and Five Precepts, requires initiates to take refuge in the Dao, Scripture, and Master Treasures as a demonstration of sincerity.
