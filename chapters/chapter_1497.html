Therefore.

Qin Sang had plenty of patience.

When he had enough strength, he would come and retrieve the treasure!

The opportunity to improve his talent was only this once, a great opportunity that would benefit him for life, so he couldn't miss it.

Bing Yao had passed on a secret technique to Dongyang Bo, allowing him to break through to the mid-Nascent Soul stage, but the essence energy in his body would decay faster, and even if he managed to hold on until the next Heavenly Tribulation descended, he would surely die.
