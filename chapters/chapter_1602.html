‘Crack!’

The Yin Puppet Crystal was placed on the platform, the lifelike puppet drawing gasps of astonishment.

Puppet Crystals are divided into Yin and Yang.

The Yang Puppet Crystal was a gift from Duke Dongyang before his death, while the Yin Puppet Crystal was obtained from the slain General Gui's Natal Puppet.

The Yin Puppet Crystal had been refined into General Gui's Natal Puppet, partially merging with it, forcing Qin Sang to seal the entire puppet.

“A puppet?”

After a brief silence, a questioning voice rose from below.

Qin Sang shifted his gaze, finding the speaker hidden within a shroud of shadows, doubly disguised, just like himself.
