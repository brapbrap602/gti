"Heh!" Nie Yun chuckled lightly. "Those who are inherently of this world have a natural reverence for it, making it much harder to break through its constraints. It was just a whim, I never expected you to actually succeed..."

"I..." Zhang Xuan blushed. "Without Kong Shi, I would never have reached this level..."

Without Kong Shi's selfless dedication, he could never have attained his current state.

"I gave him the opportunity, but he didn't seize it. His battle with Lingxi was the perfect chance for him to break through, but he chose to retreat. He thought he had a backup plan and could escape unscathed, but he actually lost his drive to improve. When facing those who transcend us, if one lacks this spirit, how can they ever contend?"

Nie Yun said.

Zhang Xuan was silent.

He had witnessed their battle; Kong Shi indeed lacked decisiveness.

Perhaps he didn't want to kill Luo Ruoxi.

Unfortunately, that single thought cost him the chance to advance.

"If Kong Shi had won, Ruoxi would have died..." After a moment, Zhang Xuan looked over, his brows furrowed.

Did this man not even care about his daughter's life or death?

"With me here, she wouldn't have died," Nie Yun smiled calmly. "Your strength is almost on par with mine now. Do you think, in a life-or-death situation, saving someone would be possible for those two?"

"That..." Zhang Xuan chuckled wryly.

The Emperor's breakthrough and the Emperor himself are two different concepts. If he truly wished to intervene, he could have easily saved the person at the last moment, ensuring they wouldn't suffer a single scratch.

"Lingxi is my other wife, Luo Qingcheng's daughter. Therefore, her assumed surname is Luo... To make her believe it and avoid emotional outbursts, she's been under the impression I'm still comatose..."

Nie Yun chuckled wryly: "I guess I've played the ruthless father role pretty well... Let you explain this to her. After all, her affections are now focused on you. I, her old father, am probably a distant memory... Haha, I'll disappear for a while. Otherwise, I'm afraid she'll raise hell..."

Seeing such an unreliable father, Zhang Xuan's face twitched. He could only agree: "Alright..."

He had no choice. After all, he had taken the man's daughter.

"The Heavenly Dao Library is a manifestation of my will, both my foundation and my constraint. Your ability to overcome this constraint demonstrates your skill and potential. Your future is limitless. As her father, I'm relieved she's with you."
