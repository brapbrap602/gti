Chapter 1672: Return to Place

As time passed, the Tianhao Ancient Mirror was no longer merely touching the surface of the seal, but had become entirely embedded within it, continuously sinking deeper. During this process, it didn't stir the slightest ripple or counterattack from the seal, as smooth as a knife through butter, a testament to the mirror's profound mystical power.

When the Tianhao Ancient Mirror penetrated the seal's interior, the scene within was unexpected. It wasn't the anticipated riot of colors, but a world devoid of light.

Dark, oppressive.

Through the Tianhao Ancient Mirror, this sensation transmitted to Fan Laomo's spirit, making him secretly apprehensive.

Soon, the Tianhao Ancient Mirror's reflection revealed that this wasn't pure darkness. There was indeed a light source. At the heart of the darkness, a single point of light flickered.

Five colors separated and intertwined, echoing the seal.

The Tianhao Ancient Mirror immediately moved towards it, and the reflection of a five-colored crown appeared.

The ancient mirror stopped beside the five-colored crown. The Ghostly Lotus within the mirror slowly drifted outwards. As the lotus drifted, its closed petals unfolded layer by layer, resuming its Myriad Illusions Ghostly Lotus form. At the lotus's heart, a mass of black qi undulated, gradually coalescing into a human silhouette – it was Fan Laomo!

At this moment, Fan Laomo's state was peculiar. He hadn't entered with his true body. Instead, he used a secret technique to transform into a Phantom Demon body, possessing the mirror, using the Tianhao Mirror as a vessel to bring his phantom form inside.

Although not his true body, and thus subject to certain limitations, many mystical powers could still be wielded by this Phantom Demon body.

*Swish!*

Fan Laomo opened his eyes, which were pitch black, with pupils resembling two Ghostly Lotuses.

"Is this that spiritual treasure?"

His black eyes shifted, a hint of doubt in Fan Laomo's gaze. Here, the five elements converged, the vital energies chaotic. His gaze fell upon the five-colored crown. Just as he was about to carefully analyze its patterns, he saw the crown tremble.

Five-colored radiance instantly illuminated all directions. Fan Laomo had a feeling that although the crown's outward appearance hadn't changed, it was utterly different from before. A supreme treasure was awakening.

In fact, Fan Laomo distinctly felt something awakening within the five-colored crown...

This something seemed to be... consciousness!

Consciousness... awakening?

This unprecedented sensation stunned Fan Laomo. Then, that feeling intensified, definitely not an illusion. In an instant, perhaps even less, a more tangible sensation emerged.

It was the feeling of being watched!

The source of the 'gaze' was the five-colored crown.

The Phantom Demon body, impervious to heat and cold, shuddered at this moment. A chill shot straight through his crown, nearly causing him to cry out, "Artifact Spirit!"

Artifact Spirit!

The five-colored crown had birthed an Artifact Spirit!

In truth, Tianhao Tower's Tianhao Ancient Mirror and Eight Sceneries View's Eight Sceneries Palace Lantern were both spiritual treasures. The presence of a spiritual treasure to suppress a sect's destiny was also a hallmark of a top-tier sect. Naturally, Sweet Dew Monastery wasn't lacking one.

But spiritual treasures differed not only in power but also in strength.

In distinguishing the strength of spiritual treasures, spirituality was a crucial factor.

When spirituality reached its peak and an Artifact Spirit was born, it marked a further transformation of the spiritual treasure.

The Tianhao Ancient Mirror and Eight Sceneries Palace Lantern had never clashed directly, and neither sect would willingly reveal their secrets. However, it was certain that both were far from birthing an Artifact Spirit.

The timeframe was difficult to calculate. It could be hundreds or thousands of years, or perhaps an Artifact Spirit would never be born.

Because even when spirituality reached its apex, it remained in a chaotic state, not necessarily capable of giving birth to a spirit. This stage was also known as the Spirit Molding stage, its difficulty comparable to a cultivator breaking through a major realm bottleneck.

Perhaps even more difficult!

Of course, Fan Laomo had gleaned this knowledge from ancient texts collected by his sect. Cultivators in the Middle Continent were numerous as the sands of the Ganges, with no shortage of Soul Transformation grandmasters, yet there were no clear records of a spiritual treasure molding a spirit.

Among this world's cultivators, very few possessed spiritual treasures. Those at the late Nascent Soul stage, like him, were still using various secret techniques to elevate their natal magic treasures to pseudo-spiritual treasures, striving towards true spiritual treasure status.

Who would dare to dream of the unattainable Artifact Spirit?

At this moment, amidst his shock, Fan Laomo suddenly understood what the Formless Immortal Sect had sealed within Emperor Bestowal Mountain.

It was to suppress an Artifact Spirit!

Even a spiritual treasure would actively choose its master, requiring the *Treasure Mastery Canon* to control. Since there was an Artifact Spirit, how could it willingly submit to a cultivator's enslavement?

Back then, when the Formless Immortal Sect wielded this treasure during the demonic tribulation, it was always accompanied by a grand formation. Thinking back now, the formations were different each time, yet all subtly aligned with the Dao of the Five Elements.

Presumably, one purpose was to wield the treasure, and the other was to suppress the Artifact Spirit, preventing it from causing chaos.

In an instant, countless thoughts flashed through his mind. Fan Laomo believed he had grasped the truth, yet his spirit remained tense, feeling the Artifact Spirit's gaze.

Raging emotions were palpable.

This was the fury accumulated from repeated failed schemes, being manipulated, and now, when the Artifact Spirit realized something was amiss, discovering that even its home had been invaded!

Under the gaze of such extreme anger, Fan Laomo's heart pounded. Seeing the jade pieces on the five-colored crown radiating brilliantly, a premonition of danger arose.

Without time to think, he instinctively waved his right hand, a dark green streak of light shooting out.

Within the streak, faintly visible were glittering grains of dark green fine sand. A green light flickered on the surface of the sand, like tiny will-o'-the-wisps, bone-chillingly sinister.

This was a treasure Fan Laomo had refined from various venomous and sinister materials, called the Filthy Yin Phosphorus Sand, specifically designed to defile magic treasures.

When using the Phantom Demon body, one could temporarily transform a treasure connected to one's spirit into a mystical power to bring inside. Fan Laomo's goal was to seize the spiritual treasure, preventing Zhu Wudao from controlling it and greatly increasing his strength. His first choice was naturally the Filthy Yin Phosphorus Sand.

Although he hadn't anticipated encountering an Artifact Spirit, it was a blessing in disguise. Using the Filthy Yin Phosphorus Sand here was opportune. However, Fan Laomo wasn't sure how effective it would be against an Artifact Spirit.

He was even less certain of the strength of a five-colored crown that had birthed an Artifact Spirit.

He coveted the five-colored crown, but he understood the principle of knowing one's limits. He didn't dare seize the treasure directly, he had to proceed cautiously.

Therefore, as the Filthy Yin Phosphorus Sand flew out, Fan Laomo retreated towards the Myriad Illusions Ghostly Lotus. He didn't want to lose this Phantom Demon body here.

*Swish!*

Fan Laomo was very close to the five-colored crown. The Filthy Yin Phosphorus Sand instantly enveloped it, the fine sand transforming into specks of filthy phosphorus fire, clinging to the crown's surface, suddenly staining it with blemishes.

The five-colored crown's luster dimmed, tainted by the dark green sinister light.

But at this moment, Fan Laomo's expression drastically changed.

Just as the Filthy Yin Phosphorus Sand was about to hit the five-colored crown, from beneath the crown, a long-hidden black tide suddenly erupted. The black tide surged, carrying a terrifying demonic intent that chilled even a great devil like Fan Laomo, fiercely impacting the five-colored crown.

It was because of this impact that the five-colored crown shuddered violently, its five-colored spiritual light wavering. The Artifact Spirit, caught off guard, couldn't react in time and was struck by the Filthy Yin Phosphorus Sand.

The black tide was the true master of the seal!

He had been used!

Fan Laomo was startled and furious. It was too late to recall the Filthy Yin Phosphorus Sand. Many questions arose in his mind.

What was the relationship between the Artifact Spirit and the black tide? If the five-colored crown was part of the seal, why hadn't the Artifact Spirit counterattacked or warned him when he entered the seal?
