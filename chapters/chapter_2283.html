"So, I sent the scattered Heavenly Dao fragments to the lowest world… bestowing them upon souls native to this world, and one soul not of this world. And you, you didn't disappoint me!" Nie Yun chuckled.

"Not of this world… so my arrival here was because of you?" Zhang Xuan was shaken. So his transmigration was orchestrated by this man.

"Hehe," Nie Yun chuckled lightly. "Those native to this world possess a natural reverence for their world, making it much harder for them to break free. It was a fleeting thought, and I didn't expect you to actually succeed…"

"I…" Zhang Xuan blushed slightly. "Without Kong Shi's selfless dedication, I wouldn't have achieved this level." Without Kong Shi's sacrifice, he wouldn't have reached this realm.

"I gave him the chance, but he didn't seize it. His battle with Lingxi was his best chance to break through; unfortunately, he chose to retreat, thinking he had a backup plan to ensure his survival. In reality, he lost his aggressive spirit. When facing those who transcend us, without that spirit, how can one contend?" Nie Yun stated.

Zhang Xuan remained silent. He witnessed their battle; Kong Shi truly lacked decisiveness. Or perhaps he didn't want to kill Luo Ruoxi. This hesitation cost him the chance to advance.

"If Kong Shi had won, Ruoxi would have died…" After a moment, Zhang Xuan looked up, his brows furrowed. Did this man not even care about his daughter's life?

"With me here, she wouldn't have died," Nie Yun smiled calmly. "Your strength is now almost equal to mine. Do you think the two of you, in a life-or-death situation, could save someone?"

"That…" Zhang Xuan chuckled wryly.

The Emperor's breakthrough and the Emperor himself are two different concepts. If he were truly willing to intervene, he could certainly save the person at the last moment, ensuring they wouldn't suffer a single scratch.

"Lingxi is my other wife, Luo Qingcheng's daughter, so her assumed name is Luo… To make her believe, and to avoid emotional entanglement, she has been led to believe I remain in a coma…"

Nie Yun gave a wry smile: "I guess I've played the role of a ruthless father… However, you should explain this to her. Her affections have clearly shifted to you, and I'm sure she's forgotten all about her old father... Haha, I'll disappear for a while. Otherwise, I'm afraid she'll cause a terrible commotion…"

Zhang Xuan's face twitched at his unreliable father. He could only agree: "Alright…"

He had no choice; after all, he had taken his daughter.

"The Heavenly Dao Library is a manifestation of my will, both my foundation and my constraint. Your ability to overcome this constraint demonstrates your potential and skill. You have a bright future. As her father, I’m relieved she's with you."
