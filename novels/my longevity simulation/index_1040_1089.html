Chapter 1040: Secrets of the Tribulation Eradication Tome

Above the gloomy grand hall, several thin threads crisscrossed.

Paper figures, each seemingly identical yet with subtle differences in their facial expressions upon closer inspection, hung neatly from these threads.

The passage of time had caused these paper figures to appear faded, turning a pale, withered yellow.

As Li Fan abruptly intruded, countless paper figures began to sway without wind, their movements eerie.

Li Fan felt an instant pressure as the gazes of all the paper figures focused on him.

As if bound by chains, Li Fan's divine soul lost its ability to act independently.

Fortunately, the swaying paper figures slowly stilled, and the pressure on Li Fan gradually lessened.

After returning to normal, Li Fan did not act rashly, as he vaguely sensed a gaze still fixed upon him from the shadows ahead.

Like a viper intently watching its prey, ready to strike at any moment, a shadowy presence slowly approached.

Li Fan continued to wait passively, letting the shadow draw nearer.

With each moment the shadow approached, the pressure on Li Fan increased.

Faint whispers reached his ears, seemingly urging him to make a move.

However, Li Fan, having observed the subtle changes around him, inwardly sneered.

During the period of静止 observation, he had already discerned the secret: the shadow's approach and the life-or-death pressure it brought were illusions, designed to force intruders into attacking the shadow.

The true form of the shadow was revealed as the gloom dissipated: a tattered rope, emitting an aura Li Fan found all too familiar—it was a restriction left by the Doctrine Transmitter Heavenly Venerate!

"The Tribulation Eradication Sect is indeed extraordinary, to have even the Doctrine Transmitter's residual restrictions corroded to this extent," Li Fan mused, his eyes narrowing as he swept his gaze across the countless paper figures in the hall.

The paper figures' faces now seemed to wear a grotesque smile, then shifted to an innocent expression.

"From the fleeting glimpse in the visions just now, it seems these paper figures are all transformed Tribulation Eradication Sect cultivators?" Li Fan thought, his mind racing. "However, the number of paper figures is a bit too many."

He released a trace of spiritual energy, attempting to pluck a paper figure from its thread. As expected, his spiritual energy passed through empty air. The paper figures in the hall appeared to be mere phantoms, devoid of physical substance.

Li Fan sent forth a wisp of his divine sense to interact with a paper figure, but still could not make contact.

"It seems one must use the Tribulation Eradication Sect's secret techniques to manipulate these paper figures."

"Destroying this restriction..." Li Fan's eyes flickered. After a moment, he changed his mind. "No, I shall repair this restriction instead!"

Li Fan found the Tribulation Eradication Sect's methods utterly distasteful. Hiding like a turtle retracting into its shell, they chose to merge with the Xuanhuang Heaven and Earth to evade calamity, relying solely on potential saviors.

Though effective, it lacked the dignity befitting one of the Ten Great Immortal Sects. More importantly, Li Fan felt inherently uncomfortable within the Tribulation Eradication Sect, as if his very nature clashed with the place, leaving him restless and uneasy.

While his separated soul remained within the hall, Li Fan's main body, outside the Tribulation Eradication Sect, began to reverse-engineer and deduce the cracking formation using the Dissolution Disc. With prior experience, it took him only seven days to successfully deduce a formation for repairing the Doctrine Transmitter's restrictions.

Li Fan, within the gloomy hall, immediately made his move.

"Creation and destruction are two paths to the same end. If I can successfully restore the Doctrine Transmitter's restriction, it means my deduction of it is correct, and I can likewise destroy it."

As if wielding a seven-colored brush, Li Fan sketched in the air. Correspondingly, the tattered rope in the center of the hall slowly regained its vitality. Light once again bathed the somber hall.

However, Li Fan's actions seemed to enrage the countless paper figures hanging above. They began to sway violently again, their faces contorted in anger, staring fixedly at Li Fan.

Instantly, a sound like the wailing of ghosts and howling of demons echoed in Li Fan's ears.

"Noisy!" Li Fan roared, utterly unfazed. He instead accelerated his repair process. The paper figures' roars could not deter him.

The rope representing the restriction reappeared from the center of the hall, extending all the way to the entrance.

With a violent tremor, Li Fan found himself back outside the hall, in the familiar empty courtyard. The hall itself had shrunk to the size of a toy, held in place by the restraining rope.

Only then did Li Fan discern the traces of the corrupted restriction hidden in the shadows. The hall continued to tremble, attempting to struggle free.

Li Fan's gaze turned cold as he continued his repair work. After the restriction in the small courtyard was repaired, the scene shifted, and Li Fan returned to the mountain path. The restriction here was the most complex.

Fortunately, most of it was still intact; only this single mountain path had been eroded by the Tribulation Eradication Sect's power. After Li Fan repaired the missing sections of the restriction, the mountain path naturally vanished.

The scene shrank again. The so-called Tribulation Eradication Sect finally revealed its true form: a book, sealed by layers of restrictions, unfurling within a void. The book contained no text, only bas-relief carvings of mountains and cultivators. As the book naturally turned its pages, the images within shifted, appearing as mere silhouettes, comical and bizarre.

Observing the book, Li Fan studied it for a long time, deep in thought.

"The Tribulation Eradication Sect was indeed annihilated by the Doctrine Transmitter. But this is precisely what they sought—seeking life amidst death, their entire sect transformed into another form of existence. This book... it's somewhat akin to a Creation Tablet. However, the Creation Tablet records the myriad things of the Xuanhuang Realm, while this Tribulation Eradication Tome only pertains to matters within the Tribulation Eradication Sect."

"Originally, that is. However, the Tribulation Eradication Sect members have all transformed into paper figures, scattered throughout the Xuanhuang Realm. Therefore, one can also, from this Tribulation Eradication Tome, glimpse the myriad phenomena of the Xuanhuang Realm through the eyes of the paper figures."

Li Fan saw fleeting glimpses of familiar Xuanhuang scenery within the rapidly turning pages and mused inwardly.

At this point, Li Fan's objective had been achieved: verifying that his formation against the Doctrine Transmitter's restriction was effective. However, looking at the Tribulation Eradication Tome, now suppressed by the Doctrine Transmitter's power, Li Fan's thoughts inevitably turned active again.
